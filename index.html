What is it about?

The purpose of this website is to assist users in becoming more organized and productive in their daily activities. Through the provision of customizable checklists, space for notes, and an integrated calendar, the site offers a comprehensive solution for personal organization. Users can create their own task lists, record important notes, and plan their time using the calendar. This combination of features provides a centralized platform for activity management, enabling users to stay focused, track their progress, and achieve greater efficiency in task completion. Make good use of it!

My tasks


    A check-list helps ensure the completion of all necessary tasks, preventing anything from being forgotten or overlooked. Additionally, it provides a systematic and organized approach, allowing tasks to be performed more efficiently and effectively. In summary, it is a valuable tool for enhancing productivity, quality, and reliability of tasks and processes. This is a space for you to create your check-list.

    Taking notes helps to organize and prioritize tasks, ensuring that nothing is forgotten, while also providing mental clarity by freeing up space in the mind to focus on ongoing activities. Notes also enable tracking progress, celebrating achievements, and identifying areas that require more attention. By reviewing the notes, it is possible to maintain focus and productivity, avoiding distractions. This is a space for you to jot down your notes.

    My annotations

    Having a calendar helps in organizing and planning activities and appointments in advance, ensuring a clear view of available time. Additionally, it allows tracking important deadlines and dates, avoiding forgetfulness and delays. This is a calendar to assist you in managing your commitments.

    The Pomodoro method is a time management technique that involves dividing work into short and focused time intervals, followed by brief breaks. By breaking the work into smaller chunks, the method helps to avoid procrastination and increase efficiency in task completion. It also aids in maintaining focus during work intervals, as tasks are performed in manageable and short time blocks. This is a Pomodoro Timer for you to manage your time and tasks effectively.
